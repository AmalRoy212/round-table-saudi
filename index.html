Overview

About the Forum

In an era of rapid digital transformation, the CXO Leaders Roundtable Forum serves as an exclusive, high-impact platform for senior executives to engage in peer-to-peer networking, benchmarking, and immersive knowledge sharing. Designed specifically for CXOs and senior decision-makers, this closed-door event fosters dialogue around Data and AI Strategy, exploring how intelligent data virtualization is transforming enterprises for AI readiness and business agility

  • Deliver Unified Data for Data Products and Self-Service Insights
    Enable seamless access to consistent data across the organization, empowering teams with faster, self-service analytics.

  • Centrally Govern and Secure from a Single Point of Control
    Simplify compliance and enhance data security by managing governance and access controls from a unified platform.

  • Improve Data Infrastructure Performance and Reduce Cloud Costs
    Optimize data pipelines and infrastructure to boost efficiency while minimizing unnecessary cloud expenditures.

  • Ensure AI Success with a Trusted Data Foundation
    Lay the groundwork for reliable AI outcomes by building on accurate, high-quality, and well-governed data.

Event Format

Each session is thoughtfully designed to deliver maximum engagement and value through the following elements:

Case Studies

Highly interactive sessions that foster open dialogue, allowing participants to exchange ideas, explore challenges, and co-create practical solutions.

Expert Presentations

Curated insights from industry leaders focusing on emerging trends, proven strategies, and best practices shaping the future of data, AI, and digital transformation.
