About the Forum

In an era of rapid digital transformation, the CXO Leaders Roundtable Forum serves as an exclusive, high-impact platform for senior executives to engage in peer-to-peer networking, benchmarking, and immersive knowledge sharing. Designed specifically for CXOs and senior decision-makers, this closed-door event fosters dialogue around Data and AI Strategy, exploring how intelligent data virtualization is transforming enterprises for AI readiness and business agility
